Our Popular Medicines

Paracetamol

Paracetamol Tablet IP 500 mg is a commonly used medication for relieving pain and reducing fever. It is widely prescribed to treat mild to moderate pain such as headaches, toothaches, menstrual cramps, muscle aches, back pain, and joint pain. It also helps in lowering fever associated with infections like the common cold or flu.

Crosin

Crosin (Paracetamol 500 mg) is used to relieve pain and reduce fever. It helps treat headaches, toothaches, muscle pain, menstrual pain, and fever due to colds or infections. It should be taken as directed, as overuse can harm the liver.

D’Cold Total is a combination medicine used to relieve symptoms of the common cold, flu, and allergies. It helps reduce nasal congestion, runny or stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, body ache, fever, and headache.

Imodium

Imodium is a medicine used to treat diarrhea by slowing down the movement of the intestines. This allows more water to be absorbed from the bowel, making the stool firmer and reducing the frequency of bowel movements.

Pilex

It helps reduce inflammation, pain, itching, and bleeding associated with piles. Pilex works by strengthening blood vessels, improving circulation, and promoting healing of the affected area.

Herbolax

Herbolax is an herbal laxative used to relieve constipation and promote regular bowel movements. Herbolax helps soften the stool, making it easier to pass, and supports overall digestive health.
